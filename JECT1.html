HotHere, Event Finder App

Human Computer Interaction, User-Centered Design, UX/UI Design

Focusing on Human Computer Interaction, this application connects users with events and venues that perfectly align with their unique style.

Development Process

Market Research

User Interviews

User Stories

Through this process, I gained valuable insights into the essential features users desire, enabling me to streamline the application for an intuitive and user-friendly experience.

Low-fidelity Prototyping

Task FLows

Wire Flows & Frames

Building on these insights, I can optimize the user experience and craft the user journey to ensure a seamless and enjoyable navigation through the app and its features.

My goal was to create an app that strikes a balance, offering simplicity for first-time users while providing robust functionality for those eager to explore its features in depth.

The UX design incorporates multiple routes allowing for flexibility while ensuring ease of return to previous states. This approach minimizes the impact of user errors, providing a forgiving and intuitive experience.

Digital Prototyping (Figma)

UI Design

Graphic Design

With my User Flows as a foundation, I developed a digital prototype, expanding on the visual aspects of the user interface to ensure a compelling and engaging user experience.

The design integrates vibrant, saturated colours and expressive iconography, harmonizing them with softer-coloured, distinct menus. This intentional blend strikes a balance, making the app visually appealing and approachable for a diverse audience, from jazz loungers to bar-hoppers.
